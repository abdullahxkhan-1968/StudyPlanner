About Us

Study Planner Dashboard is a smart and user-friendly platform designed to help students organize their academic activities in an efficient way. Our goal is to make study management simple, productive, and stress-free. Students can manage subjects, create personalized study schedules, track learning progress, and organize daily tasks in one place. The dashboard helps users stay focused on their goals by providing an organized environment for planning studies and improving productivity. With features like subject management, timetable organization, progress tracking, and task planning, students can easily monitor their performance and build better study habits. Our mission is to create a learning experience that encourages discipline, consistency, and better time management for every student.
